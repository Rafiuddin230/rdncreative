Background

Define

Define

Displays Menus

Presenting the restaurant menu with a clear and appetizing appearance, as well as providing complete information about each dish.

Order Process

Make the ordering process intuitive and fast so that customers can easily select food, adjust their selections, and add them to their cart.

Order Tracking

Provides an order tracking feature that informs customers of the status of their food preparation and delivery.

Secure Payment

Ensuring safe and comfortable payment processes, and providing various payment options such as credit cards, digital payments, or direct payments.

Ideation

Solution

Displays Menus

Use appetizing, high-quality food images in menu displays. Organize menus with clear categories and group dishes according to type.

Order Process

Display food and drinks with clear prices. Provides an "Add to Cart" button next to each dish. Allows users to customize options such as portion sizes or extras.

Order Tracking

Provides a tracking feature that indicates the order status, from confirmation to delivery. Provides notifications when an order is ready to be picked up or shipped.

The Result

The UI/UX Mobile App Restaurant Food application has succeeded in providing an easy and comfortable experience for customers in ordering food and drinks. Customers can browse menus easily, make choices according to their preferences, and make payments securely. This application helps restaurants to improve ordering efficiency, provide positive experiences to customers, and increase customer loyalty to restaurants.
