Tech Stack

Background

The Municipal Government of Bekasi has an official website that functions as a center for information and public services for residents, entrepreneurs and related parties. However, today's website may face challenges in terms of optimal display, navigation and user experience. The aim of this project is to redesign the UI/UX of the Bekasi government website to improve accessibility, readability, and ease of use.

Rebuilding the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) of the Bekasi City government website.

This application will help customers to explore menus, place orders, and make payments easily.

The Challenge

Challenge

Unattractive Appearance

The current website may have an unattractive and modern appearance, which may affect user impressions.

Complex Navigation:

The Solution

Solution

Modern Visual Design:

Uses a modern visual design with a clean layout, appropriate colors, and eye-catching design elements to create a professional and modern impression.

Simple Navigation

Simplify navigation with well-structured menus, information groupings, and clear category choices.

Important Information

Provides a tracking feature that indicates the order status, from confirmation to delivery. Provides notifications when an order is ready to be picked up or shipped.

Present important

Present important information such as announcements, news, services and contacts in a way that is easily accessible and emphasized.

The Result

Through the UI/UX redesign of the Bekasi City government website, the new website will provide a better and more efficient user experience. Citizens and related parties will find it easier to find the information they need, interact with the government, and use the services provided. This website will reflect the professionalism and openness of the government, and help build better relations between government and society.
